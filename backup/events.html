College of Technology General Assembly

This year's General Assembly at PLM Tanghalang Bayan was a major success, with students and professors alike turning out in large numbers to participate in the activities and events that were offered.

The photobooth was a popular attraction, with many attendees taking the opportunity to snap fun and memorable photos with their friends and colleagues. Mini-games and an amazing race were also held, providing opportunities for attendees to engage in friendly competition and have some fun.

Overall, the General Assembly was a fantastic opportunity for the PLM community to come together and celebrate their shared interests and passions.

GDSC General Assembly

The Google Developer Students Club (GDSC) at the University of PLM is a student-led organization that provides opportunities for members to learn about Google technologies, collaborate on projects, and engage with the local tech community through workshops, hackathons, speaker events, and other activities.

The GDSC is a great way for students at the University of PLM to connect with other tech-minded individuals and get involved in the local tech scene.
