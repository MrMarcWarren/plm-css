The PLM CSS

The PLM CSS is Founded during 2018. The CSS is the Main Student Organization of the Computer Science Department. PLM Computer Science Society is an Academic Organization of, for and by the Computer Science Students of Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Maynila that aims to provide skills and Knowledge in different topics in Computer Science for the Benefits of its members.
